Privacy Policy for kubik

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Introduction

kubik ("the Game") is a free-to-play color block puzzle game. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Game.

Information we collect

kubik does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

The Game stores your high score and preferences locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

kubik displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include device identifiers, IP address, and general location data. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Advertising Policies.

Third-party services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob — for serving advertisements. AdMob's privacy policy can be found at AdMob Privacy.

Children's privacy

kubik is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The Game's ad configuration is set to not serve personalized ads to users identified as under the age of consent.

Data retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain. Game data (scores, preferences) is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
